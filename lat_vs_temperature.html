Temperature

The above scatter plot vizualizes how a world city's latitute is related to the city's temperature. As mentioned above, the equator sits at 0° latitude, and based off what we can see in this plot, as a city's latitude gets closer to 0°, it's maximum temperature is greater. Whereas city's whose latitude is further from 0°, have lower maximum temperatures. Therefore, it can be concluded based on the data visualization, that city's that are closer to the equator are warmer than cities that are further away.
